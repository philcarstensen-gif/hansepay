How to add to Gmail
  1. Click Copy Signature above — the signature is copied to your clipboard.
  2. In Gmail, click the ⚙ Settings icon (top right) → See all settings.
  3. Stay on the General tab and scroll down to Signature.
  4. Click Create new, give it a name (e.g. "HansePay"), then click inside the signature text area.
  5. Press Cmd + V to paste.
  6. Scroll to the bottom and click Save Changes.
