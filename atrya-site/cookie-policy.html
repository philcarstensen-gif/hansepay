Cookie Policy

Caplend Technologies GmbH · Last updated: May 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We also use similar technologies such as localStorage — browser-based storage that works like cookies but is never sent to a server. This policy covers both.

What we use

Atrya's website uses only the minimal storage necessary to function. We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

Name Type Purpose Expires
atrya-theme localStorage Remembers your light or dark mode preference so it persists across visits. Until cleared
atrya-cookies localStorage Records that you have acknowledged this cookie notice so the banner is not shown again. Until cleared

Third-party services

We load fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, Google may log your IP address and browser information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. No cookies are set by Google Fonts directly.

We do not embed analytics, advertising networks, social media widgets, or any other third-party tracking scripts.

Legal basis

The storage items listed above are strictly necessary for the website to function as you expect and do not require separate consent under Art. 25 TTDSG and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. You may clear them at any time via your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

How to manage or delete storage

You can clear all locally stored data through your browser's settings. In most browsers: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data. You can also use your browser's developer tools to inspect and delete specific entries.

Blocking all local storage may prevent your theme preference from being saved, but the site will continue to work normally.

Changes to this policy

If we introduce new cookies or storage in the future we will update this page and, where required by law, seek fresh consent. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
