01 — Brand Story

About Atrya

Atrya is Europe's first regulated on-chain FX layer and native payment service provider (PSP) — built at the intersection of traditional finance and blockchain-native settlement infrastructure. Developed by Caplend Technologies GmbH, Atrya enables businesses, fintechs, and financial institutions to settle cross-border payments with the speed, transparency, and programmability of on-chain technology, while operating fully within Europe's regulatory framework. Where legacy FX infrastructure is fragmented, opaque, and slow, Atrya is unified, auditable, and instant.

The name Atrya is derived from the ancient Sanskrit root meaning "one who transcends" — reflecting the company's ambition to transcend the boundaries of traditional financial infrastructure. The brand is designed to convey precision, trust, and technological sophistication: a bridge between the rigor of regulated finance and the radical efficiency of distributed ledger technology. Every element of the Atrya visual identity — from the hexagonal mark to the typographic choices — communicates clarity, structure, and forward motion. Atrya does not merely process payments; it redefines what payment infrastructure can be.

02 — Logo Usage

Logo Variations

The Atrya logo consists of two elements: the hexagonal mark (icon) and the "Atrya" wordmark set in Syne SemiBold. They may be used together as the full logo, or independently as needed. The primary logo is always the full lockup on dark background. Use light variants only on light-colored backgrounds.

Logo Do's & Don'ts

Do
  • Use the full logo lockup as the primary identifier
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "A" on all sides
  • Use dark logo on dark backgrounds, light logo on light backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or squish
  • Use SVG format for screen; vector formats for print
  • Ensure sufficient contrast between logo and background
Don't
  • Change the logo colors outside of approved brand palettes
  • Add drop shadows, bevels, or other effects to the logo
  • Rotate or flip the logo
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Rearrange the icon and wordmark positions
  • Use the wordmark alone if the mark has not already been shown

05 — Iconography

The Atrya Mark

The Atrya mark is a hexagon containing a centered diamond. These two geometric forms carry specific meaning: the hexagon represents structure, precision, and network connectivity (echoing both blockchain architecture and the molecular bonds of regulated infrastructure). The inner diamond represents the exchange transaction — the crossing of value at a precise intersection point. Together, they communicate that Atrya is a structured, precise, and connected exchange layer.

07 — Brand Voice

Tone & Voice

Atrya's brand voice reflects the company's positioning: at the intersection of rigorous financial regulation and cutting-edge technology. We speak with precision and confidence, but never with jargon for its own sake. We are a trusted infrastructure provider — not a consumer fintech app. Our voice is measured, expert, and forward-looking.

Precise. Not technical.

Use clear, exact language. Avoid buzzwords and vague claims. If you can't explain it clearly, you don't understand it well enough.

Confident. Not arrogant.

We are building something genuinely new. State it as fact. Don't oversell — the product speaks for itself.

Institutional. Not cold.

We communicate with the gravitas of a regulated financial institution, but we remain human and accessible to the partners we serve.

Forward. Not futuristic.

We are building now, not in theory. Talk about what we do and what we enable today, not science-fiction applications.

Minimal. Not sparse.

Say more with less. Every word earns its place. No filler. No padding. Headlines should be statements, not questions.

European. Not provincial.

We are a European company operating globally. Regulatory compliance is our competitive advantage, not a constraint.

Tone in Practice

Do say

"Atrya settles cross-border FX transactions on-chain, with full regulatory compliance — in seconds."

Don't say

"We're disrupting the payments industry with our revolutionary blockchain-powered super-app!"

Do say

"Payment logic. Settlement. Unified." — clear, structured, final.

Don't say

"We make payments faster, easier and more fun for everyone!" — vague, consumer-facing, not our register.
